# FIFA World Cup 2026 Tournament Format and Rules

**Source**: [FIFA World Cup 2026 Regulations (Official PDF)](https://digitalhub.fifa.com/m/636f5c9c6f29771f/original/FWC2026_regulations_EN.pdf)

---

## 1. Group Stage Structure

### Number of Teams and Groups
- **48 teams** total in the final competition
- **12 groups** with **4 teams per group** (Groups A through L)
- Host countries are pre-assigned:
  - **A1**: Mexico
  - **B1**: Canada
  - **D1**: USA

### Match Format
- Round-robin format within each group (each team plays the other 3 teams once)
- **3 matches per team** in group stage
- Points system:
  - Win: 3 points
  - Draw: 1 point
  - Loss: 0 points

### Match Schedule Pattern
```
Match Day 1: X1 vs X2, X3 vs X4
Match Day 2: X1 vs X3, X4 vs X2
Match Day 3: X4 vs X1, X2 vs X3
```
- Last two matches in each group have **simultaneous kick-off times** on the same day

---

## 2. Qualification from Groups to Knockout Stage

**32 teams advance** from the group stage:
- **24 teams**: Top 2 from each group (12 group winners + 12 runners-up)
- **8 teams**: Best 8 third-placed teams (out of 12 third-place finishers)

## 4. Third-Place Team Qualification Rules

The **8 best third-placed teams** are determined by (in order):
1. Greatest number of points in all group matches
2. Goal difference in all group matches
3. Greatest number of goals scored in all group matches
4. Highest team conduct score (fair play - yellow/red cards)
5. FIFA/Coca-Cola Men's World Ranking (most recent)
6. Previous FIFA Rankings (if still tied)

**495 possible combinations** exist for which 8 third-placed teams qualify and their R32 matchups (detailed in Annexe C of the regulations).

---

## 5. Tiebreaker Rules (Article 13)

### Within Groups (Step 1 - Head-to-Head)
For teams equal on points:
1. **Points** in matches between tied teams
2. **Goal difference** in matches between tied teams
3. **Goals scored** in matches between tied teams

### Step 2 - If Still Tied
If teams remain tied after Step 1, apply criteria to remaining tied teams only:
4. **Goal difference** in ALL group matches
5. **Goals scored** in ALL group matches
6. **Team conduct score** (Fair Play):
   - Yellow card: -1 point
   - Indirect red (2 yellows): -3 points
   - Direct red: -4 points
   - Yellow + direct red: -5 points
   - (Highest point total = best ranking)

### Step 3 - If Still Tied
7. **FIFA/Coca-Cola Men's World Ranking** (most recent edition)
8. **Previous FIFA Rankings** (iteratively until decided)

---

## 6. Extra Time and Penalty Shootout Rules (Article 14)

### Knockout Matches
- If tied after 90 minutes: **Extra time** (2 x 15-minute periods)
- 5-minute interval before extra time (players remain on pitch)
- No break between the two extra time periods (short drinks break allowed at half-time of extra time)
- If still tied: **Penalty shootout** per Laws of the Game

### Coin Toss Procedure
1. First toss: Determines which goal is used (unless safety/pitch concerns)
2. Second toss: Winner chooses to take first or second kick

---

## 7. Substitution Rules (Article 36)

### Regular Time
- Maximum **5 substitutes** per team
- Maximum **3 substitution opportunities** (excluding half-time)
- Half-time substitutions do not count as an opportunity
- If both teams substitute simultaneously, counts as one opportunity each

### Concussion Substitution
- One additional **permanent concussion substitution** allowed per match
- Does not count against the 5-substitute limit
- Opposing team receives one additional regular substitution if this is used

### Extra Time (Additional Rules)
- One **additional substitute** allowed (6th substitute)
- One **additional substitution opportunity** (4th opportunity)
- Substitutions before extra time starts: Does not count as opportunity
- Substitutions at half-time of extra time: Does not count as opportunity

---

## 8. Match Duration
- **90 minutes** total (2 x 45-minute halves)
- **15-minute half-time interval**
- Cooling breaks may be implemented for extreme weather conditions

---

## 9. Yellow Card Reset
- Single yellow cards are **cancelled after the quarter-finals**
- Two yellow cards in different matches = automatic one-match suspension
- Direct red card = automatic one-match suspension (plus potential additional sanctions)

---

## 10. Key Tournament Dates
- **Tournament period**: June 11 - July 19, 2026
- **Mandatory player release**: May 25, 2026 (day after last club match on May 24)
- **Team arrival**: No later than 5 days before first group match
- **Minimum rest between matches**: 3 days (except between semi-finals and third-place match)
